Trigonometry

====================

The branch of mathematics with is called trigonometry is preeminently a subject
devoted to measurement and numerical calculation.

Trigonometry = triangle and measure from Greek
Was originally a branch of geometry


The opening between two straight lines drawn from the same point is called an
angle.

The general geometric properties of the circle are studied in geometry, and the
value of the number pi is there calculated from the relation of the radius and
diameter of a circle to its circumference, the definition of pi being the
ration pi = C/d.
