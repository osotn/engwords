SDLC: software development lifecycle.
Development: plan, code, build, test
Operation: deploy, operation, monitoring

Deployment: Process of installing application in server.
Operation: updating.

Devops = dev + ops

Devops = methodology, it is a not a tool, technology or frame work.
The logo of DevOps represents infinite because its a never ending continuous process.

Waterfall methodology: - also known as the Waterfall model or classic model - is a
sequential development process to develop the software systematically.
It will follow step by step execution, we can work on one step at a time.
