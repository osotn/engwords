simple  present  questions

We  use  do or does  in  simple  present  questions:

Study  the  word  order:

What  do  you  do?  I  work  in  a  bank.

Do  they  like  music? 
Does  he  like  music?

Short answers

Do  you  play  tennis?  No,  I  don't.
Do  your  parents  speak  English?  Yes,  they  do.
Does  Gary  work  hard?  Yes,  he  does.
Does  your  sister  live  in  Vancouver?  No,  she  doesn't.
