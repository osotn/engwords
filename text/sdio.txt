Secure Digital = SD
on MultiMediaCards (MMCs)

Integrated WiFi - several companies produce SD cards with build in WiFi transveivers supporting static security.
The card lets any digital camera with an SD slot transmit captured images over a wireless network, or store
the images on the card's memory until it in in range of a wireless network.

These devices can use the SD slot to support:
	- GPS receives;
	- modem
	- barcode readers
	- FM radio
	- TV tuners
	- RFID readers
	- digital cameras
	- WiFi, Bluetooth, Ethernet and IrDA

It is now more common for I/O devices to connect using the USB interface.
