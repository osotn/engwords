
God created in 7 days:
    -   the heavens;
    -   the earth;
    -   light;
    -   separated light (day) from darkness (night);

    -   vault to separate water (heaven, sea) from water (land);
    -   plants that bear seed;
    -   living creatures: cattle, creeping;
    -   human beings in his own image from the dust of the ground;

Eden:
    -   the tree of life;
    -   the tree of the knowledge of good and evil.

Put the man into a deep sleep and, took one of the man's ribs and closed up
the flesh. Built up a woman.

Serpent was the most cunning of all the creatures.

Man and woman ate apples from tree of knowing good and evil.
And they eyes were opened, and they knew that they were naked.

The man lay with Eve and she conceived and gave birth to Cain.
Then another child - Abel.

Abel tended the flock, Cain worked the land.

Cain attacked and murdered his brother.

Cain lay with his wife, and she conceived and gave birth to Enoch.
Enoch -> Irad -> Mehujael -> Methushael -> Lamech.

Lamech married two women: Adah and Zillah.
Adah gave birth to Jabal. Zillah bore Tubal-cain.

Eve gave birth to a son, and named him Seth.

Noah was a righteous man.
He had sons: Shem, Ham, Japheth.

Ham -> Canaan.

People want to make bricks build a city and a tower with its tip in the heavens.
God created a lot of languages. Babel city.

Abram and Sarai traveled to Egypt.
Abram said that Sarai is his sister, not his wife.

Abram was a father of many nations.

Sodom and Homera.

God tested Abraham to kill this only one child.

Jacob worked seven years for Laban to marry his younger daughter Rachel.
But Laban gave Leah daughter instead of Rachel.
And after the next seven years gave Rachel.
