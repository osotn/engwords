03B
===
We use the continuous for temporary situations (things that go on for a short time around now).
We use the simple for permanent situations ( things that go on for a long period of time).
I am living with some friends until I find a place of  my own.
My parents live in London. They have lived here all their life.
We are working hard today. Yes, I have a lot to do.
John is not lazy. He works hard most of the time
